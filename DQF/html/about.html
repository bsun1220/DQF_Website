What is Quantitative Finance?

Quantitative finance is the usage of mathematical models and large data sets to analyze financial markets and securities. Quants tend to specialize in various areas including derivatives pricing, algorithmic trading, investment management, and market-making. This finance industry heavily focuses on mathematical, statistical concepts and their applications in computer science.

Education

DQF provides a 6 week-long training program held every semester to teach the basics needed to enter into quantitative finance. First, we teach the basics of trading games. Next, we go over financial markets concepts as well as the place that quantitative trading firms have in the world. Finally, we wrap it up with data science topics and real-world applications.
